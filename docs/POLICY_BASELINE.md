# Policy Baseline v0 (Prototype)

## Community guidelines baseline
- SFW-only platform behavior.
- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination are not allowed.
- No coercive behavior or unsafe meetup conduct.
- Respect privacy and boundaries.

## Moderation process (draft)
1. Report intake (member/host/lead).
2. City Lead triage.
3. Regional Monitor escalation for high-severity cases.
4. Admin final review for account removals or multi-city bans.
5. Action + rationale written to audit log.

## Refund/cancellation assumptions (draft)
- Standard window: full refund up to 72 hours before event start.
- Partial refund logic to be finalized per experience type.
- Scholarship credits restored on eligible refunds.

## Legal placeholders
- Terms of Service: pending legal review.
- Liability waiver template: pending legal review.
- Incident response ownership: product + regional operations.
